Buritto

Description

An easy to make and delicious recipe from Mexico. It's all you want to eat wrapped in a tortillia and ready for you to enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Tortillia Wrap
  • Beans
  • Corn
  • Rice
  • Guacamole
  • Minced Meat
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Wash the Corn and the beans.
  2. Let the rice cook.
  3. Season your meat and put in the pan.
  4. Take the meat and the rice of the heat.
  5. Heat up the tortillia in the oven.
  6. Wrap all the Ingredients in your Wrap.
  7. Enjoy!
