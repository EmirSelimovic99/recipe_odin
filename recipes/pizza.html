Pizza

Description

This delicious italian Meal consists of dough, cheese and tomatosauce. The toping can change to whatever you would prefer. No matter if you like the classic Magharitta or Pizza Hawaii, you're in for a treat.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Flavour
  • Mozarella
  • Tomatoesauce
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix up the flavour with water and salt.
  2. Let it sit for 30 min.
  3. Stretch the dough out.
  4. Put the sauce on evenlly on top alongside your topings.
  5. Put the Pizza in the oven. 45 min at 180c should do the trick.
  6. Enjoy!
